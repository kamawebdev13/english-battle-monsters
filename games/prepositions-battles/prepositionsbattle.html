Prepositions Battle

How to Play?

  • You will see a sentence with a missing word (___).
  • Choose the correct preposition to complete the sentence.
  • You have 3 options. Click the correct one.
  • You have 10 seconds to answer each question.
  • If your answer is correct, you win points and your monster Prepo grows.
  • If your answer is wrong, you lose points and your monster gets smaller. and Meaner grows
  • The game has several questions.
  • Try to get the highest score.
  • At the end of the game, write your name to save your score.
  • Click on the button below. Learn English and have fun!
